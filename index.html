First‑Principles Focus


Here you’ll find interactive Python notebooks, mathematical deep dives, and hands‑on explorations that trace machine learning from its historical roots to modern neural networks. The focus here isn’t just on using ML tools — it’s on understanding them from first principles. Whether you're studying linear regression, unpacking the geometry behind gradient descent, exploring the evolution of early perceptrons, or breaking down neural networks into their core mathematical components, this hub is built to make every concept transparent and intuitive.


I invite you to learn alongside me. This hub grows as I grow — one concept, one experiment, and one breakthrough at a time.
